What is UF SG?

UF Student Governemnt (UF SG) is modeled after the US Government with three branches- Executive (Student Body President, Vice President and Treasurer), Legislative (Senate) and Judicial (Supreme Court).

However, UF SG isnâ€™t necessarily run as a ethically as a government should be. On the contrary, UF SG has a long history of scandals and underlying issues with corruption. This website seeks to explore the highlights of these problems.

A Quick Note

This website will refer to certain UF SG parties as either "The System" or "Independent" (or Indies). The System party refers to the group that has the most power and is systematically (proven through this website's research) set up to win elections for years. The Independent parties, on the other hand, are generally started by individuals, and tend to die out after a few years when those most involved gradaute. The System party is almost always the majority, and tends to have scandals every few years (detailed in Recent History) that leads to the party rebranding itself and act like it's a new one, when in reality it tends to have mostly the same members as the previous one. This Alligator article explains the Unite to Swamp party transition.
